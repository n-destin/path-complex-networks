paper review: JK-networks (Jumping Knowledge Networks)

- Learning adaptive range of neighborhods to aggregate features from based on the distribution of the Influence of other nodes on a specific node
- The Influence score of node x by another node y, is the sum of all
    absolute values of the jacobian of the derivative of the final representation of the node with respect to the initial representation
    of that node. 

- and we normalize it the score to define a distribution. 
- So we have junmp connections from each aggregation layer to the final layer, and we carefully select which 
representations to choose (use for final output prediction)
